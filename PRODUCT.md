# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

People in Torrent and the Valencia area who are considering psychological or educational support for themselves, their partner, or their children. Visitors arrive with mixed intent: some need individual therapy, others seek child psychology, logopedia, pedagogy, or couples and family therapy. They are often uncertain, comparing options, and looking for a clinic that feels safe and competent before taking the step to call or visit.

## Product Purpose

Present Clínica 21 Días Psicoeducación as a trusted local clinic with a full range of therapeutic and educational services. The site must help visitors understand what the clinic offers, feel confident in its professionalism, and take action by contacting the clinic via WhatsApp or phone, or by feeling ready to visit in person. Success is measured by informed, low-friction contact and lasting trust in the clinic's credibility.

## Brand Personality

Warm and professional. The voice is approachable and human without sounding casual or vague. The clinic should feel like a place where specialists listen and explain clearly, not like a hospital ward or a wellness influencer page. Calm confidence over urgency; clarity over jargon.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS landing pages: cream or sand backgrounds, gradient text, startup hero metrics, identical icon-card grids, and templated marketing copy. The site should not read as an AI-generated template for "any healthcare brand."

## Design Principles

- **Trust before contact.** Every section should earn confidence; the call to action follows proof, not the other way around.
- **One clinic, many needs.** Speak to adults, parents, couples, and families without siloing the experience into separate audiences on the homepage.
- **Warm professionalism.** Human and inviting, never cold or institutional, never vague or overly spiritual.
- **Local credibility.** Ground the clinic in Torrent and Valencia without feeling small or provincial.
- **Specific over generic.** Name real services, real outcomes, and real ways to get in touch; avoid buzzwords and empty promises.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard web accessibility best practices: sufficient color contrast, keyboard navigation, visible focus states, meaningful link and button labels, and reduced-motion alternatives for any animation. No formal WCAG compliance target yet; design decisions should not create barriers for common assistive technology use.
